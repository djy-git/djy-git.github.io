# 1. Notations
$$
Y = (Y_1, \cdots, Y_n)^T \in \mathbb{R}^n \\
X = (x_ij) \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times p} \\
\beta = (\beta_1, \cdots, \beta_p)^T \in \mathbb{R}^p
$$

# 2. Model
$ Y \mid \beta, \sigma^2 \sim N_n(X\beta, \sigma^2 I_n) $
