### Model
$ likelihood: \ f(\mathbf{x} | \theta_1, \theta_2, \theta_3) $
<br>

### Prior
$ \pi(\theta_1), \pi(\theta_2), \pi(\theta_3) $
<br>

### Full posterior
$$ \pi(\theta_1, \theta_2, \theta_3 | \mathbf{x}) = \frac{f(\mathbf{x} | \theta_1, \theta_2, \theta_3) \cdot \pi(\theta_1, \theta_2, \theta_3)}{\int_{\theta}{f(\mathbf{x} | \theta_1, \theta_2, \theta_3) \cdot \pi(\theta_1, \theta_2, \theta_3)} d\theta} \newline \newline
\quad\quad\quad\quad \propto f(\mathbf{x} | \theta_1, \theta_2, \theta_3) \cdot \pi(\theta_1, \theta_2, \theta_3)
$$
<br>

### Full conditional posterior
$$
\pi(\theta_1 | \theta_2, \theta_3, \mathbf{x}) = \frac{\pi(\theta_1, \theta_2, \theta_3 | \mathbf{x})}{\pi(\theta_2, \theta_3 | \mathbf{x})}
$$
