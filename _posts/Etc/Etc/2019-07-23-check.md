Unbiased estimator $\hat{\beta}$ of parameter $\beta$
$ E[\hat{\beta}] = \beta $
$ E[X\hat{\beta}] = X\beta $

<br>

$$
Y = f(X) + \epsilon = X\beta + \epsilon \\
\hat{Y} = \hat{f}(X) = X\hat{\beta} \\
$$

<br>

Bias = $ (X\beta - E[X\hat{\beta}])^2 = 0$
