## 1. Maximum Likelihood Estimate (ML, MLE)
### 1) Notations
- **Data** <br>
(Input, Output) : $ (x_{1..S}, \ y_{1..S}) $

- **Parameter** <br>
$\theta$

- **Probability Density** <br>
$ f_\theta(x_i) $

- **Assumptions** <br>
i.i.d. Condition: Independent Identically Distributed

### 2) Likelihood
- **Single data** <br>
$ p(y_i | f_\theta(x_i)) $

- **Multiple data** <br>
$ L(\theta) = \Pi_i p(y_i | f_\theta(x_i)) $

- **Log likelihood** <br>
$ l(\theta) = \sum_i log p(y_i | f_\theta(x_i)) $
